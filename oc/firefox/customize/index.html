An Add-on for Everyone

Firefox has the most ways to customize your online experience specifically for the way you use the web. There are more than 5,000 Add-ons (little extras that augment Firefox to meet your unique needs) just waiting out there to help you do more, have more fun and be more creative online.

Want some specifics? Check out the examples below, then visit our Add-ons site for the full range of possibilities.

Alerts & Updates

With Add-ons from this category, you get the latest on the weather, sports or even eBay auctions. Real time info shows up right in your browser, no matter which site you happen to be on.

Photos, Music & Video

Turn your Firefox into a photo uploader, a music player and an all-around multimedia powerhouse. It’s like having a media control panel at your fingertips while you surf, work, email or research on the web.
