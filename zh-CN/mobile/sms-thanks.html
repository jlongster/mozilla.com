Thanks for choosing Firefox!

Follow these steps to download Firefox, learn more about Firefox Sync, and be on your way

  1. Check your phone’s SMS inbox for the text we just sent you.
  2. Open the text and select the embedded link to download Firefox directly to your mobile.

*Firefox is free to use; however, standard carrier data rates may apply. Please check your wireless plan.

While you’re waiting…

  1. Setup Firefox Sync and take your Firefox with you wherever you go. Share your browsing history, bookmarks, passwords and tabs on your PC with your mobile.
  2. Install the Firefox Sync add-on on your PC and sign in to Firefox Sync. Once Firefox downloads to your mobile, follow our suggested Next Steps and select "Get the Firefox Sync add-on" to complete the install. Alternatively, you can manually select the settings button, select the add-ons button, then search for and install the Firefox Sync add-on on your mobile.
