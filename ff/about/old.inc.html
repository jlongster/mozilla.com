Ena moƴƴi e Geese.
Ena moƴii e Winndere.

Mozilla wonaa fedde topotoonde gaadanteere. Ko min renndo huuɓngo daraniingo topaade jaaɓnirɗe Alla meho, tawa ko "open source" ɓeydanooje mo woni kala juɓɓugol kuutorgol geese e jookli winndere ndee kaka. Ko min dokkotooɓe, baŋŋinooɓe, ƴeewtotooɓe, e wasiyotooɓe ummiiɓe e nokuuji winndere ndee kala ngam gollaanaade yoo Geese internet duumo e wonde nokku denndaaɗo mo alanaa hay gooto njoɓdi. Amin ngoongɗiɗi wonde topagol udditiigol ena tiiɗtina cuɓagol e kesamhesamaagu etee mo woni kala, e nokku kalal ena haandi e ko ɓuri hisde, ko ɓuri yaawde e ko ɓuri yuɓɓude e kuutorgol Geese.

Topirɗe amen nawɗe raay e teknologii amen ena keɓoo e aduna oo kala e ko ɓuri ɗemɗe 70 tawa hay taŋka yoɓaakaa.

Jooɗorde Mozilla woni ko to Mountain View, California wonndude e birooji diiwanuuji to Auckland, Beijing, Copenhagen, Paris, Tokyo and Toronto.
