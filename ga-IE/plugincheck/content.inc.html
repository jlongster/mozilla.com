Ceisteanna Coitianta

Cad is forlíontán ann?

  • Plugins power videos, animation and games.
  • They're built outside of Firefox by companies like >Adobe Systems and >Apple.
  • Plugins don't always update automatically.

Cén fáth ar chóir dom mo chuid forlíontán a nuashonrú?

  • Old plugins can interrupt browsing and waste your time.
  • Old plugins increase your risk for attack by malware, viruses, and other security threats.
  • Updated plugins have improvements that make the web better and safer for you.

Conas is féidir le Firefox teacht i gcabhair orm?

Amach anseo, déanfaidh Firefox nuashonrú ar do chuid forlíontán go huathoibríoch. Go dtí sin, ba chóir duit filleadh ar an leathanach seo go minic agus do chuid forlíontán a nuashonrú le do shlándáil a chosaint.

Cé hiad na forlíontáin atá agam?

Bhraitheamar do chuid forlíontán thuas go huathoibríoch; chun na forlíontáin atá agat a fheiceáil i bhFirefox lean na treoracha seo:

  1. Oscail an roghchlár Uirlisí.
  2. Roghnaigh Breiseáin.
  3. Cliceáil an cluaisín Forlíontáin.

Cén chaoi a ndíchumasóinn forlíontán?

I bhFirefox:

  1. Oscail an roghchlár Uirlisí.
  2. Roghnaigh Breiseáin.
  3. Cliceáil an cluaisín Forlíontáin.
  4. Cliceáil forlíontán sa liosta.
  5. Cliceáil an cnaipe Díchumasaigh.

Rabhadh: disabling a plugin means that you will no longer be able to do certain things. For example, if you disable Flash, you will not be able to watch videos on popular video streaming sites.
