Getting Started

  1. Download Home

    Download Home - Free

  2. Download Firefox and restart Firefox

    Firefox Home uses the capabilities of Firefox Sync, a built-in feature in Firefox for desktop and mobile, to access your Firefox browser information and securely send it to your iPhone.

    Download Firefox and restart Firefox.

  3. Set up Sync

    From your desktop, create a Sync account by navigating to Tools » Set Up Sync » and select Create a New Account. Follow the steps, creating a username, password and Sync Key.

  4. Login on your iPhone

    Launch Firefox Home on your iPhone and enter your new Sync account login info and tap Done.

    Firefox Sync will begin securely synchronizing your data with our service. This initial synchronization may take a few minutes. If you're having trouble logging in, click here for help.

    For further help and support, see our Firefox Home Support site.
