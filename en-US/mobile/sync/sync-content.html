Keep Your Life In Sync

Securely access your Firefox history, bookmarks, tabs and passwords across your devices.

Get Started with Firefox Sync

  1. Install the Firefox Sync add-on and set up your account on your PC.
  2. Install the Firefox Sync add-on directly to your device.
  3. After install, enter your Firefox Sync account info and select “Sync Now”.

Step 1. Install

Start by downloading Firefox 4 for desktop. Then, restart Firefox and create a Sync account by going to the top menu and selecting Firefox » Options (Win) or Preferences (Mac) » Sync.

Step 2. Sync Up

Follow the steps to create a Sync account, providing an email and password. You’ll also be asked to provide a Captcha to ensure you’re not a robot. Watch the short video for extra assistance.

Watch the demo »

Step 3. Connect

Once you have a Sync account, it’s time to connect your devices.

Mobile:

Swipe to Tools » Preferences and tap on Setup Sync. A single-use Sync code will appear. Then go to the Firefox menu on your desktop, select Firefox » Preferences » Sync » Activate New Device and enter your code.

Firefox Home:

Download and launch Firefox Home. Tap on Setup Firefox Home and a single-use Sync code will appear. Then go to the Firefox menu on your desktop, select Firefox » Preferences » Sync tab » Add New Device and enter your code.

Stay Safe

Firefox Sync works behind the scenes to keep you safe.

For Your Eyes Only:

Your data is always protected, so only you have access to information like your passwords and browsing history.

Safety First:

Firefox Sync is the only service with end-to-end encryption. Sync’s advanced security measures mean you’re never vulnerable to online bad guys or companies that will sell your information.

Get Up and Go:

Enjoy the benefits of sharing personal info across all your devices while still maintaining military-grade security and privacy.

For Older Versions

If you’re still using Firefox 3.5 or 3.6, Sync isn’t a built-in feature but you can still make it work. First, download the add-on to your desktop, then restart Firefox and follow the steps to create a Sync account.
