Keep Your Firefox In Sync

Seamlessly sync your Firefoxes, access your data no matter which device you use.

Just follow these simple steps:

  1. If you haven’t already, download the latest version of Firefox for desktop.
  2. Find Sync in the Preferences menu on your Mac or go to Options in the Firefox menu in Windows.
  3. Create a Sync account with an email address and password.
  4. Select Add a Device and follow the instructions.

That’s it! Now you can take your Firefox with you wherever you go.
