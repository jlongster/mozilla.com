Supported Devices and System Requirements

Firefox is available for Android phones (2.0 and above) and the Nokia N900.

Supported Devices

Below is a list of tested devices compatible with Firefox.

Maemo

Android

  • Firefox is compatible with Android 2.0 and above devices.
  • Installation requires about 17 MB internal or SD card storage.
