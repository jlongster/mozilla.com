Stay in Sync

Access your history, passwords, bookmarks and even open tabs across all your devices.

To go from desktop to mobile without interruption, create a Firefox Sync account on your desktop and follow the steps to setup all your devices. Learn more and get started using Firefox Sync.

Imagine an Awesome Bar (Smart Location Bar) that knows your Web history regardless of which device you visited it on – home desktop, work laptop, or mobile.

Leverage years of desktop browsing on the first day you fire up your mobile. Even your saved passwords from your desktop can be used to automatically fill out forms.

Surf the Web on your desktop, get up and go, and have your open tabs ready and waiting for you on your mobile – just as you left them on your computer.

Your information is encrypted from end-to-end so only you can access it. Firefox puts security as a top priority and syncing is no exception. Learn More

Type less, Browse More

The Awesome Screen pops up just by tapping on the Awesome Bar (location bar). It gets to know you and gives you instant access to a smart list of your favorite history items, bookmarks and tabs from your other computers.

Tap on the magnifier next to the Awesome Bar and send your search directly to Google, Wikipedia, Amazon.com or Twitter with just one tap. Customize your default search engines to fit your lifestyle.

Privacy & Security

Customize your settings for passwords, cookies, loading images and installing add-ons for a fully empowered mobile browsing experience

  • Instant Web site ID ("Larry") lets you tap on a site favicon for an instant identity overview
  • The Password manager lets you choose to remember site passwords and avoid long and difficult typing
  • Popup blocker banishes pop-ups from your surfing experience once and for all

  • Receive Automated Update notifications as soon as updates to Firefox are available. Install them right away, or wait until you're ready
  • Forget Password setting in the Site Menu tells a site to forget your password if you want to share your phone or no longer trust a site

Performance & Productivity

Optimized for power and efficiency, Firefox for mobile is built on the same browser engine as Firefox for the desktop, letting you interact with and enjoy your favorite sites

  • Offline browsing in Firefox lets you have a Web connection even if your mobile device doesn't have one. Certain enabled sites may allow applications—like your Web-based email—to stay with you at all times
  • Memory Management in Firefox uses less of your mobile phone’s memory while it’s running so you can browse all your favorite sites with speed
  • Downloads Manager lets you download any file to your mobile. It will download in the background while you continue browsing, enables multiple simultaneous downloads, and lets you to stop, pause or resume downloads
