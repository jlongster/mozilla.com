Welcome to Mozilla Spaces

Expanding our presence globally and bringing Mozillians together worldwide.

Mozilla Spaces are open working environments where Mozillians can hack, code, design, research, create, engage and contribute to building a brighter future for the Web. Keep reading to learn more or check out the map above for info on specific locations.

Why Mozilla Spaces?

  • To increase participation by providing opportunities to interact, learn and collaborate with Mozilla
  • To attract, recruit and retain paid talent and volunteers by enhancing the Mozilla experience
  • To make more people aware of Mozilla and to increase our influence in local communities

Where will you find Mozilla Spaces?

  • Areas with a concentration of active and enthusiastic Mozillians
  • Places with influential Web development, artistic and cultural climates
  • Bike-friendly locations close to transportation, restaurants and cultural hot spots

Stay tuned for info on future spaces and how you can help propose a space near you.

What can you expect from Mozilla Spaces?

  • Workstation seating, hoteling areas for contributors and a community space for meetups
  • Wireless Internet access and video conferencing to keep you connected with our other spaces
  • Unique Mozilla culture with regional flair
