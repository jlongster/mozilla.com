Congrats!

Now that you have a Firefox Sync account, securely access your Firefox history, bookmarks, tabs and passwords across your devices.

Sync your Android

  1. Step 1.

    Install Firefox for Android by visiting Firefox.com/m on your phone’s default browser, or install it over-the air.

  2. Step 2.

    Launch Firefox on your phone, swipe to Tools » Preferences and tap on Connect. A single-use sync code will appear.

  3. Step 3.

    Then come back to the Firefox menu on your desktop, select Firefox » Options (Win) Preferences (Mac) » Sync » Add New Device and enter your sync code.

Sync your iPhone

Download and launch Firefox Home. Tap on Setup Firefox Home and a single-use sync code will appear. Then go to the Firefox menu on your desktop, select Firefox » Preferences » Sync » Activate New Device and enter your sync code.

For more detailed instructions, visit setting up Firefox Sync on your Android or Maemo, or setting up Firefox Home on your iPhone.
