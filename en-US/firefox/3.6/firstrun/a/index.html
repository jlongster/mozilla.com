Get Tips & Tricks

Check out these tips & tricks to make the most out of your Firefox experience.

  • Beginner: Zoom In, Zoom Out

    Tired of tiny text? Want to see an image a little more up close and personal? Now you can easily zoom in and out of Web pages.

  • Intermediate: Play Tag

    Keep your bookmarks organized by assigning tags to your bookmarked sites: just double-click on the star icon and you’ll be prompted to enter your tags.

  • Advanced: Go Directly to Your Favorite Sites

    Add keywords to your bookmarks for easier and faster access. From the Library, just add a short keyword in the keyword field, and you’ll be able to access that bookmark by simply typing that keyword into the address bar.
