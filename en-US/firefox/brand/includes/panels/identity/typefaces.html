Typefaces

If you’re the kind of person who’s passionate about x-heights, kerning and descenders — you know, a font nerd — you’re in good company here. Even if you’re not, here’s a list of the fonts we use at Mozilla, including their use cases and some other tasty tidbits.

Meta Bold

The old, trusted Mozilla standard, Meta Bold is reserved for our Mozilla wordmark and usage at the Foundation level. It’s our rock.

Meta Medium

At the product level, Meta Medium is our champion and creates a second tier of Meta font usage. Sleek, svelte, nimble.

Meta Normal

Our third tier of font usage features Meta Normal as the light and lean choice for sub-branding. Light in weight, heavy in awesome.

Meta Black

Reserved for our Web headlines, Meta Black goes above and beyond its font family siblings and throws its weight around to get noticed.

Georgia

A classic typeface, Georgia is another standard font used in various settings and functions for both Web and print.

Recommended Open Fonts

When it comes to typography at Mozilla, we love supporting the Open Font community. We’ve selected a few we go to regularly to share with you here.
