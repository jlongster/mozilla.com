The Many Faces of Mozilla

Get to know the different parts of the Mozilla project, and find out how you can help!

Addons.mozilla.org

Discover thousands of ways to customize your Mozilla software, or start coding and submit your own Add-on.

Bugzilla

The comprehensive database of every bug or enhancement request filed for Firefox, Thunderbird and other Mozilla projects.

Mozdev

This community-powered site provides free project hosting for Mozilla-based applications and extensions.

Mozilla Developer Center

The online hub for all things related to development of Firefox, Thunderbird and other Mozilla software.

Mozilla Labs

A virtual lab where people come together to create, experiment and play with new Web innovations and technologies.

Mozilla Messaging

The home of our efforts to redefine email and online communication through Thunderbird and beyond.

Mozilla.org

Home of the Mozilla community and a gateway to all community projects and resources.

MozillaZine

This community-powered site contains news, forums, help and blogs about the Mozilla Project.

Planet Mozilla

Wondering what’s going on in the Mozilla community? Check out this feed of blog posts from all around the Mozilla world.

QMO

Where the QA team and community work together to test and improve Firefox and other Mozilla projects.

Spread Firefox

Home of Mozilla Community Marketing, and the place to be if you want to help tell the world about Firefox.

Support.mozilla.com

Firefox’s community-supported help center. Find the answers to your questions, or share your expertise to aid others.
