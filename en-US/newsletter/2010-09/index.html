Take Firefox with You

Stay in Sync
Do you use Firefox at work and at home? Firefox Sync securely connects all your computers so you can access your bookmarks, passwords, browsing history and even your open tabs from any computer. All it takes is a quick download of the free Firefox Sync add-on and you're ready to go.
