Get the most out of Firefox & eBay

Keep an eye on your eBay trading wherever you are on the web when you install the Mozilla Firefox eBay-Edition. It’s a free tool built with eBay users in mind that will help you get more out of your buying and selling.

Be a better bidder

You're always securely signed in so you can check out your progress whenever you like. Customize your alerts so you stay on top of your bids. Be told when an auction style format listing you’re bidding on is about to end.

Get updated, instantly

Never miss the eBay action. No matter where you're browsing, and whatever you're doing online, you can get an at-a-glance update on all your eBay activities: watching, buying or selling.

Safer browsing

You're safer with the Firefox Companion. You'll know you're on a genuine eBay page and not a spoof site or lookalike. When you're using Firefox you're also at less risk from pop-up ads, spyware and other web threats.
