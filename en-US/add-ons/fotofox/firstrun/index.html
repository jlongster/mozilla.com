Welcome to Fotofox!

Fotofox has been added to your copy of Firefox, so you're almost ready to start uploading pictures to an online photo album. Make sure you're connected to the Internet and then follow these easy steps:

  1. Connect Fotofox to your Online Photo Albums
    Click on the large orange button to connect Fotofox to your favorite online photo album. Select the service from the list, and click "Continue" to either log in or create a new account for yourself.
  2. Select photos to upload using Fotofox
    Click on the "Add" button or just drag a picture from your computer into Fotofox. You'll see a thumbnail preview of the picture, and can add a title, descriptive "tags" and pick an album for the picture to be placed into online.
  3. Click "Upload"
    That's it! Sit back as your pictures are sent to your online photo album. When the transfer is finished, just click "View" to be taken to the web page and see your photos!

You can use Fotofox with any of the following online photo album services:

  • Kodak EasyShare Gallery
  • Flickr
  • Tabblo
  • Marela
  • 23
  • SmugMug
