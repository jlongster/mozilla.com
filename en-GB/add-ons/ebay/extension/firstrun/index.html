You're about to discover why millions of people worldwide have made the switch to Firefox for a better, faster, more secure online experience. Firefox is developed and supported by Mozilla, a global community working together to make the Web a better place for everyone.

To start using your Firefox Companion for eBay, just follow these easy steps:

  1. Click on the eBay icon on your Firefox toolbar and a sidebar will open in your browser.
  2. Login to your eBay account by clicking on the “Connect to eBay” button in the sidebar and entering your account information in the main browser window. If you are a new eBay user, click on the Register button to get started.
  3. That’s it! You’re ready to search and monitor the millions of items available on eBay everyday.

For more information about Firefox Companion for eBay features and commonly asked questions, check out the features page and FAQ.

Feel secure with the eBay companion and Firefox. Our community of more than 100,000 testers is working every day to make you’re protected against all the latest online security threats.

Make Firefox your default web browser

For the complete experience, click the Preferences option in the Firefox menu above and make sure “Always check to see if Firefox is the default browser on startup” is checked. That way you’ll be getting the benefits of Firefox at all times, even when you click a link in an email.
