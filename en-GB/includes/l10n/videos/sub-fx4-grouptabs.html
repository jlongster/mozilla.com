How to organize your tabsTab sets give you the ability to see a visual overview of all of your tabs…
…and group them into sets, then you can organize your workflow and keep separate tasks separate.
As you've already discovered you can use this tab in the tab strip to enter tab sets.
You can then click on a tab, or hit the escape key to go back.
Of course you can hit any tab in here to zoom to that tab.
Alternatively, you can use option-space on Mac or control-space on Windows to enter and exit tab sets.
To group tabs simply drag two together.
So in this case, I want to group all of my pictures of space.
Alternatively, I can click and drag in empty space to make a group.
When you zoom into a set, you only get those tabs that are in that set.
To organize your tab sets, simply drag the tab sets around.
You can change their size so that more important things are bigger…
…and less important things are smaller.
If you make a tab set small enough, it will turn into a pile.
