Why Get Firefox Sync?

Typing is hard

Let’s face it, typing on your mobile is difficult. A bid farewell to typing long URLs and search terms when the Awesome Bar remembers your history, bookmarks and tabs from both your PC and mobile, quickly getting you where you want to go and with very little typing.

Don’t start from scratch

Say goodbye to resetting and retyping. Firefox Sync syncs your Firefox passwords and preferences, so you can hit the ground running.

Get up and go

Take your Firefox with you wherever you go. Surf the Web on your PC, get up and go, and have everything waiting for you on your phone: your history, open tabs, bookmarks, the Awesome Bar - just as you had left it.

Getting Started

  1. Install Firefox Sync on your PC

    Make sure your PC runs Firefox 3.5 or higher and your phone is a Firefox-tested device (upgrade Firefox).

    • Download Firefox Sync
    • After the download is complete, click “Restart Firefox” to finish installing the Firefox Sync Add-on.
    • When Firefox restarts, use the “Tools” menu or the Firefox Sync icon on the bottom-right of the status-bar to open Firefox Sync and sign in.

  2. Install the Firefox Sync add-on on your mobile

    • Go your Preferences, select the add-ons button.
    • Search for and select the Firefox Sync add-on. Sign in, sync, and away you go.
