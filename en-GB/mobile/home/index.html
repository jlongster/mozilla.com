Getting Started

  1. Install the free Firefox Sync add-on on your desktop.

    Firefox Home uses the sync capabilities from the Firefox Sync add-on to access your Firefox browser information and securely send it to your iPhone.

  2. Once Firefox restarts, follow the prompts to create an account with both a password and a Secret Phrase.

    Firefox Home uses your Secret Phrase to locally encrypt and protect your information.

  3. Firefox Sync will now begin securely synchronizing your data with our service. This initial synchronization may take up to 20 minutes.

    Firefox Sync will only work if you are not in Private Browsing mode. Once your initial synchronization is complete, you'll see a Last Update time in the Tools > Sync menu.

  4. To complete the setup, on your iPhone enter your account, password and Secret Phrase in the field provided and tap "Done" on the keypad.

    If you're having trouble logging in, click here for help.

  5. Once complete you will be able to access your desktop bookmarks, history, and open tabs on your iPhone.

    If your data is not showing up, click here for help.
