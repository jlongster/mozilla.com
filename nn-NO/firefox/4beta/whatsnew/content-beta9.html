Help us polish Firefox 4!

Firefox 4 Beta needs more in-depth testing from users like you! To help us polish this release before launch, take 30 seconds each day to help us test out the following features. You can send us a quick report to tell us how we're doing through the feedback button on the top right corner of your browser.

Site Compatibility

Does your favorite site work? Help us make sure that the sites you visit all the time work properly on Firefox 4. Can you login to website accounts? Can you stream videos? Can you play your favorite games? Let us know!

Sync

If you have Firefox Home for iPhone or Firefox 4 Beta for Mobile installed on your mobile phone, try syncing your browser session across your devices. Can you log-in to your account properly? Are your bookmarks synced?

  • Learn more about Sync »
  • Learn more about Firefox Home »

Panorama

Have you tried Panorama yet? Click on the Panorama icon on the top right of your browser. Are all of your open tabs appearing? Can you easily move them around the pane? Are they behaving the way you would expect them to? Let us know!

Plugin Compatibility

Plugins help power videos, animations and games on popular websites. Plugins, made by third party companies, can sometimes crash because of compatibility issues. Are streaming music and videos playing correctly? Can you easily chat with your friends on social networking sites? Let us know!
